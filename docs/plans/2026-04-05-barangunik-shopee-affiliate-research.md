## 6. Price Sweet Spot Analysis

### Indonesian Impulse Buy Psychology

**Key Research Findings:**
- Impulse buying accounts for **40-80%** of all purchasing activity in Indonesia
- 74% of Indonesian online shoppers have experienced regret from impulse purchases (Slickdeals)
- Hedonic motivation (excitement, joy) is MORE powerful than price discounts for triggering impulse buys
- BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later/PayLater) significantly increases impulse purchases
- Scarcity ("stock terbatas") and serendipity ("baru nemu") drive impulse decisions

### Optimal Price Tiers for BarangUnik

| Tier | Price Range (Rp) | Buyer Psychology | Commission Est. |
|------|------------------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Micro** | 5K-15K | "Cuma segini, beli aja" - zero hesitation | Rp 175-525 |
| **Sweet Spot** | 15K-35K | Best conversion zone - feels like a deal | Rp 525-1,225 |
| **Premium Impulse** | 35K-50K | Still impulse, but needs "wow" factor | Rp 1,225-1,750 |
| **Stretch** | 50K-99K | Needs justification (review/demo helps) | Rp 1,750-3,465 |

**Recommendation:** Focus on **Rp 20K-45K** range.
- High enough for decent affiliate commission (3.5% base = Rp 700-1,575 per sale)
- Low enough that people buy without overthinking
- Matches TikTok impulse buy sweet spot ($1-3 = Rp 15K-50K)

### Commission Calculation

Shopee Affiliate Indonesia (2026):
- Base commission: up to **3.5%** per sale, capped at Rp 7,000
- Komisi XTRA from sellers: additional **5-40%** on select products
- Average realistic commission per Rp 30K product: Rp 1,050 (base) to Rp 7,000+ (with XTRA)
- **Target:** 100 sales/day = Rp 105K-700K/day = Rp 3.15M-21M/month

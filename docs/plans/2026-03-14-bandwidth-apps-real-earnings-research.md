# Bandwidth & Node Sharing Apps — Real Earnings Research (March 2026)

Based on actual user reports, blog reviews, payout proofs, and forum discussions.
All figures are per **single residential IP / single device** running 24/7.

---

## TIER 1: Actually Worth Running ($5-20/month per device)

### 1. Honeygain (Baseline) — $5-15/month
- **Rate:** ~$0.10/GB + content delivery bonus
- **Realistic earnings:** $5-15/month single device (user reports: "$90/year with 0 referrals")
- **Min payout:** $20 (PayPal, BTC, JumpToken)
- **Status:** LEGIT, PAYS RELIABLY
- **Red flags:** Slow to reach payout threshold; some Reddit users call it "not worth the electricity"
- **Verdict:** Reliable baseline. Best in US/EU locations.

### 2. EarnApp (Bright Data) — $5-10/month
- **Rate:** $0.0138/active hour (US) / $0.0069/hour (rest of world). Since Aug 2025, pays per active hour not per GB.
- **Realistic earnings:** Up to $10/month per US IP, $5/month non-US
- **Min payout:** $10 (PayPal, Wise, Amazon gift card)
- **Status:** LEGIT, PAYS RELIABLY (owned by Bright Data, a major proxy company)
- **Red flags:** Changed payment model in 2025; hard cap per IP now
- **Verdict:** Top tier. Backed by Bright Data = reliable payments.

### 3. Pawns.app (formerly IPRoyal Pawns) — $5-10/month
- **Rate:** $0.20/GB (double Honeygain's rate)
- **Realistic earnings:** ~$7/month (real payout proof: $7.33 Dec 2025)
- **Min payout:** $5 (PayPal, BTC, USDT, virtual Visa)
- **Status:** LEGIT, PAYS RELIABLY
- **Red flags:** None significant; rates are genuinely higher than most
- **Verdict:** One of the best. Low threshold, good rates.

### 4. Repocket — $3-6/month
- **Rate:** $0.20/GB
- **Realistic earnings:** ~$6/month sharing 1GB/day
- **Min payout:** $5-$7 (PayPal, Wise, USDT Polygon)
- **Status:** LEGIT but complaints about withdrawal rejections
- **Red flags:** Reports of payouts being canceled due to "irregular bandwidth earnings" after users hit threshold. 1M+ users, 4.9-star rating but some serious withdrawal complaints.
- **Verdict:** Decent but watch out for payout cancellation issues.

### 5. Mysterium Node (MystNodes) — $2-30/month
- **Rate:** Paid in MYST tokens (currently ~$0.16/token)
- **Realistic earnings:** Highly variable. One reviewer: $2.33/month. Top sellers: much higher. Realistic average: $5-15/month for residential IP in good location.
- **Min payout:** No minimum (on-chain MYST tokens, Polygon network)
- **Status:** LEGIT, ESTABLISHED PROJECT
- **Red flags:** Earnings extremely location-dependent; MYST token price volatile
- **Verdict:** Good if you're in a high-demand location (US, EU). Residential IPs earn significantly more than datacenter.

### 6. Proxyrack — $5-20/month
- **Rate:** $0.50/GB (highest per-GB rate in the industry)
- **Realistic earnings:** $5-20/month depending on how much your IP is used
- **Min payout:** $5 (PayPal only)
- **Status:** LEGIT
- **Red flags:** Earnings entirely dependent on whether customers choose your IP; can be $0 some months
- **Verdict:** High rate but unpredictable. Good as a supplement.

## TIER 2: Low but Legitimate ($1-5/month per device)

### 7. PacketStream — $1-5/month
- **Rate:** $0.10/GB (unchanged for years)
- **Realistic earnings:** $0-$15/month, most users report $1-5/month
- **Min payout:** $5 (PayPal, JumpTask)
- **Status:** LEGIT BUT VERY LOW EARNINGS
- **Red flags:** One user: "took almost a year to reach $5." Another review titled "Used for 8 weeks, feels worthless."
- **Verdict:** Legitimate but barely worth running alone. Only useful stacked with other apps.

### 8. PacketShare — $1-5/month
- **Rate:** $0.10/GB
- **Realistic earnings:** $1-3/month single device (claims $10-30/month but overstated)
- **Min payout:** $20 (PayPal, JumpTask)
- **Status:** LEGIT
- **Red flags:** High $20 payout threshold for low earnings = months to cash out
- **Verdict:** Similar to PacketStream. High threshold makes it frustrating.

### 9. TraffMonetizer — $1-3/month
- **Rate:** $0.10/GB
- **Realistic earnings:** $0.01-$0.10/day = $0.30-$3/month realistically
- **Min payout:** $10 (BTC, USDT, PayPal)
- **Status:** LEGIT BUT BARELY EARNS
- **Red flags:** Antivirus flags it as PUP. Referral income > bandwidth income (pyramid-ish). You can't control how your bandwidth is used.
- **Verdict:** Bottom tier. Most users quit after weeks of near-zero earnings.

### 10. EarnFM — $1-12/month
- **Rate:** Claims ~$6/GB (way above market — likely inflated marketing)
- **Realistic earnings:** Wildly inconsistent. Some: $12/month. Others: "$0.19 after 3 weeks."
- **Min payout:** $15 (PayPal, Amazon, USDT)
- **Status:** LEGIT BUT INCONSISTENT
- **Red flags:** Huge variance in user reports. High payout threshold. App crashes.
- **Verdict:** Could be decent in premium countries (US/UK/CA). Don't count on it.

### 11. Bitping — $1-5/month
- **Rate:** Per-job basis (network testing tasks, not bandwidth sharing)
- **Realistic earnings:** $1-5/month for most locations
- **Min payout:** Paid in SOL (Solana) — no minimum, on-chain
- **Status:** LEGIT, UNIQUE MODEL
- **Red flags:** Transitioned to Solana-only payouts (Feb 2025). Earnings very location-dependent.
- **Verdict:** Interesting model (testing, not proxying). Low but consistent. Good if you want SOL.

### 12. ByteLixir — $2-10/month
- **Rate:** Per bandwidth shared
- **Realistic earnings:** Company claims $24-48/month average (2023-2025 data) but this seems optimistic. Realistic: $2-10/month.
- **Min payout:** $2 (instant crypto withdrawal)
- **Status:** LEGIT
- **Red flags:** Limited user reviews available. Claims seem inflated.
- **Verdict:** Low minimum payout is nice. Worth trying as a supplement.

## TIER 3: Peer2Profit — AVOID

### 13. Peer2Profit — AVOID
- **Rate:** Varied by location
- **Realistic earnings:** Was $1-5/month when operational
- **Min payout:** $2 (was lowest in industry)
- **Status:** WEBSITE SHUT DOWN (mid-2023). Migrated to Telegram bot only. New registrations closed.
- **Red flags:** Website closed. Operations moved entirely to Telegram. Some users got paid via Telegram bot, others couldn't cash out. Classic signs of a dying/dead platform.
- **Verdict:** DO NOT START. Platform is effectively dead for new users.

## TIER 4: DePIN / Crypto Token Projects (Speculative — $0 until token has value)

These pay in points/tokens, NOT direct USD. Earnings are speculative and depend on token price after TGE.

### 14. Grass (GetGrass) — SPECULATIVE
- **How it works:** Browser extension shares bandwidth; earn Grass Points
- **Token status:** GRASS token LIVE on Solana. Current price: ~$0.37 (down from ATH $3.90)
- **Airdrop 1:** 100M tokens distributed to 2.8M users (Oct 2024)
- **Realistic airdrop value:** Highly variable. Average user may have gotten ~35 tokens = ~$13 at current price. Early heavy users got significantly more.
- **Airdrop 2:** Expected H1 2026
- **Red flags:** 90% price decline from ATH. Token unlock (181M tokens Oct 2025) created sell pressure.
- **Verdict:** The biggest DePIN bandwidth project. Token is live and tradeable. Worth running for Airdrop 2 if you're already doing this stuff, but don't expect life-changing money.

### 15. Nodepay — DEAD TOKEN
- **How it works:** Browser extension collects browsing signals + bandwidth
- **Token status:** NC token launched Jan 2025. Price crashed 99%+ from $0.33 to $0.00087
- **Realistic value:** Essentially worthless now
- **Red flags:** 99% price crash. Now requires staking NC tokens to earn rewards (running extension alone no longer earns). Classic death spiral.
- **Verdict:** AVOID. Token is practically dead. Staking requirement means you'd be throwing money away.

### 16. Gradient Network — NO TOKEN YET
- **How it works:** Browser extension (Sentry Node) — earn EXP points
- **Token status:** NO token yet. No TGE date announced. Points are NOT crypto and NOT transferable.
- **Funding:** $10M from Multicoin, Pantera, Sequoia (June 2025) — good backers
- **Red flags:** No guarantee points convert to anything valuable. Could be years.
- **Verdict:** Strong VC backing makes it worth running speculatively. Zero guaranteed earnings.

### 17. Teneo Protocol — NO TOKEN YET
- **How it works:** Browser extension earns Teneo Points
- **Token status:** NO token yet. TGE expected Q2 2026. Estimated listing price $0.03-$0.07.
- **Funding:** $3M from RockawayX, Borderless Capital, CertiK
- **Red flags:** No token yet. Speculative. Many DePIN projects never launch tokens.
- **Verdict:** If TGE happens at $0.05, early participants might get $5-50. Speculative.

### 18. Koii Network — LOW VALUE TOKEN
- **How it works:** Desktop node runs compute tasks, earns KOII tokens
- **Token status:** KOII token live (TGE Jan 2025). Price crashed 98.7% from ATH.
- **Total paid to operators:** $3M total across all operators (as of Jan 2026)
- **Red flags:** Token price crashed. $3M divided among thousands of operators = very little per person.
- **Verdict:** Running. Paying. But token value tanked. Maybe $1-5/month at current prices.

### 19. Titan Network — NO CLEAR EARNINGS YET
- **How it works:** Desktop node shares storage/bandwidth, earns TNT tokens
- **Token status:** TNT token exists but limited exchange listings. Mainnet was targeting Q2 2025.
- **Red flags:** Unclear earnings structure. Limited real user reports. Electricity cost $2-5/month may exceed earnings.
- **Verdict:** Too early to evaluate. Run only if you're already deep in DePIN speculation.

## IMPORTANT CAVEATS

1. **Location matters enormously.** US/EU IPs earn 2-5x more than Asia/Africa/South America.
2. **These rates assume 24/7 uptime.** Laptops that sleep earn much less.
3. **Running multiple apps on same IP is fine** — they don't conflict.
4. **Electricity cost:** ~$2-5/month for a mini PC running 24/7. This eats into profits.
5. **Privacy risk:** Your IP is used as a proxy exit node. Someone could browse illegal content through your IP. This is the real cost of these apps.
6. **ISP ToS:** Some ISPs prohibit reselling bandwidth. Check your terms.
7. **Antivirus warnings:** Normal for proxyware apps. Not necessarily malware but be aware.
